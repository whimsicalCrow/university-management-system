Title: US-042 Finalise Documentation and Pilot Support
Persona: Department Administrator

Description:
As the department administrator I want complete documentation and pilot support materials so that faculty and students can adopt the platform smoothly.

Acceptance Criteria:
- User guides for professors and students cover login, updates, meetings, and notifications.
- Technical runbook details deployment, environment configuration, and rollback procedures.
- Pilot feedback survey and support contact details are distributed to participants.
- Go-live checklist is completed with sign-offs from stakeholders.

Tasks:
- Draft and review user manuals with screenshots or annotated walkthroughs.
- Update technical documentation to reflect final architecture and operational steps.
- Prepare pilot communication plan including survey and support channels.
- Conduct go-live readiness review and capture sign-off records.
